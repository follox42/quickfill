## Detailed Description

### QuickFill — Smart Form Auto-Filler

Tired of typing your name, email, and address into every form? QuickFill fills them for you — in one click.

**How it works:**
1. Create profiles (Personal, Work, Test...)
2. Open any page with a form
3. Click "Fill" or press Alt+F — done!

**Smart Field Detection**
QuickFill automatically detects 17+ field types:
- Name (first, last, full)
- Email & Phone
- Address (street, city, zip, state, country)
- Company & Job Title
- Username & Website
- And more...

It works by analyzing autocomplete attributes, field names, labels, and placeholders — matching with 95%+ accuracy.

**Multiple Profiles**
Create separate profiles for different contexts:
- 👤 Personal — your real info for shopping & services
- 💼 Work — company email, office address, business phone
- 🧪 Test — dummy data for development and QA

Switch between profiles in one click from the popup.

**Works With Any Framework**
QuickFill uses native browser event dispatching, so it works perfectly with:
- React (including controlled inputs)
- Vue.js
- Angular
- Plain HTML forms
- Dynamic SPAs (detects new forms automatically)

**Keyboard Shortcut**
Press Alt+F on any page to fill the form instantly with your active profile. No need to open the popup.

**Privacy First**
- All data stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.sync)
- NO servers, NO accounts, NO tracking
- NO data collection whatsoever
- Your information never leaves your device

**Ultra Lightweight**
- Only 88KB total — won't slow down your browser
- Fills forms in under 100ms
- Zero impact on page load time

**Free Plan Includes:**
- 3 profiles
- All 17+ field types
- Keyboard shortcut (Alt+F)
- Right-click context menu
- Dark mode support

**Pro Plan (coming soon):**
- Unlimited profiles
- Per-site rules (auto-select profiles)
- Cloud sync across devices
- Import/Export profiles
- Priority support
