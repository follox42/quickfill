# Privacy Policy — QuickFill Chrome Extension

**Last updated:** March 26, 2026

## Overview

QuickFill is a browser extension that helps you fill web forms automatically using locally stored profiles. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data Collection

**QuickFill does NOT collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers.**

All profile data (names, emails, addresses, etc.) is stored exclusively in your browser using Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.sync` API. This data syncs between your Chrome browsers only if you are signed into Chrome, using Google's own encrypted sync infrastructure.

## What We Store (Locally Only)

- Profile names and icons
- Form fill data you manually enter (name, email, phone, address, etc.)
- Your settings and preferences (active profile, animation toggle)

## What We Do NOT Do

- We do NOT collect analytics or usage data
- We do NOT use cookies or tracking pixels
- We do NOT transmit data to any server
- We do NOT have access to your browsing history
- We do NOT read or store passwords
- We do NOT inject advertisements
- We do NOT sell or share any data

## Permissions Explained

- **storage**: To save your profiles and settings locally in Chrome
- **activeTab**: To detect and fill form fields on the current tab when you click "Fill"
- **scripting**: To inject the form detection and filling script into web pages

## Third-Party Services

QuickFill does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

## Data Deletion

To delete all QuickFill data:
1. Right-click the QuickFill icon → "Remove from Chrome"
2. Or go to chrome://extensions → Remove QuickFill

All locally stored data is permanently deleted when the extension is removed.
