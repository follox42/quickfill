# Product Hunt Launch Copy

## Tagline (< 60 chars)
Fill any form in one click -- free forever

## Description (< 260 chars)
QuickFill auto-fills web forms instantly. 3 profiles, 17+ field types, keyboard shortcut, and smart detection that actually works. Built because Lightning Autofill (500K users) went paid. Privacy-first: your data never leaves your browser. Open source.

## Maker Comment (First Comment)

Hey Product Hunt!

I built QuickFill out of frustration. I was a daily user of Lightning Autofill -- a Chrome extension with 500K+ users for auto-filling forms. Then they switched to a paid model ($5-10/month), and their Chrome Web Store reviews went from glowing to furious overnight.

I figured: filling in your name and email shouldn't cost $10/month. So I spent a few weeks building a replacement from scratch.

The result is QuickFill -- a Chrome extension that detects form fields intelligently (using labels, placeholders, attributes, and autocomplete hints) and fills them in one click. It's 88KB, builds in 9ms, and makes zero network requests. Your data stays on your machine.

The free version covers what most people need: 3 profiles, 17+ field types, and a keyboard shortcut. There's a Pro option for power users who want more.

I'd love your feedback -- especially on the field detection. It currently scores 22/22 on standard field types, but real-world forms are messy and I want to make it bulletproof.

Thanks for checking it out!

## 5 Key Features

- **One-click form filling** -- Fill entire forms instantly with a single click or keyboard shortcut (Alt+Q)
- **Smart field detection** -- Analyzes labels, placeholders, names, IDs, and autocomplete attributes to detect 22 field types automatically
- **3 free profiles** -- Switch between personal, work, and testing data in seconds
- **Privacy-first** -- Zero network requests, no analytics, no tracking. All data stored locally in your browser
- **Lightweight & fast** -- 88KB total size, 9ms build time, no bloat. Built with TypeScript and Manifest V3
